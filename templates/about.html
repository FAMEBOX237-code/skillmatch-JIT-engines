Our Story

It all started in a university hostel with a simple frustration...

We watched brilliant students struggle to find help with assignments while others had skills sitting unused. One student needed a logo. Another could design it — but they never met.

We saw talented minds with groundbreaking project ideas — solar chargers, campus apps, study tools — but no way to fund prototypes or find teammates.

“Why do we pay strangers online when the perfect collaborator is sitting three rooms away?”

That question became SkillMatch+ — a platform where students trade skills, fund real projects, and turn ideas into reality — together.

From a late-night hostel idea to a movement of 0+ students across Cameroon, we’re just getting started.

Late-night planning session · January 2025

Our Mission

What we do every single day

To connect students with complementary skills, find the exact help they need for academic and personal projects, eliminate the barriers between great ideas and execution, and create a self-sustaining ecosystem where knowledge and resources flow freely — no cash required, just passion and talent.

Skill exchange · Project funding · Real impact

Our Vision

Where we’re going

To become the primary catalyst for student innovation platform in Africa — where every student project that can change a campus, a community, or a country finds the skills, teammates, and funding it deserves.
